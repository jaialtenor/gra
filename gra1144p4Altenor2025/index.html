Media and Performances

The Adrienne Arsht Center celebrates the best of theater, dance, and live performance, offering audiences unforgettable experiences that bring Miami’s creative spirit to life.

Chicago: The Musical

A timeless Broadway hit full of glamour, rhythm, and unforgettable choreography that brings the roaring 1920s to the Arsht stage.

Miami City Ballet

Celebrating artistry, elegance, and emotion, the Miami City Ballet continues to shine as one of the premier dance companies in the country.

Jazz Roots Live

An Arsht Center tradition — a night where music, storytelling, and culture meet under the lights of the Knight Concert Hall.

Visit the Arsht Center

Located in downtown Miami, the Adrienne Arsht Center stands as one of the largest performing arts centers in the United States. With its stunning architecture, vibrant programming, and prime location along Biscayne Boulevard, the Arsht Center is the heart of Miami’s cultural scene.
